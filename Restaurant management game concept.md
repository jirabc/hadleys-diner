Hadley's game is live! Here's how to play:

**How it works:**
1. **Orders appear** at the top — each one shows the patron's name, what they want, which table they're at, and a patience bar that drains over time
2. **Click an order** to select it (it highlights blue), then **click the right kitchen station** to start cooking
3. **Watch the station** fill up as it cooks — when it's done, the order flips to "Ready!"
4. **Click the table** in the dining room to deliver the food and earn points

**Chaos events** fire every 12–20 seconds — stations catch fire, spills reset cooking, new parties walk in out of nowhere!

**Scoring:** Deliver fast while the patron is still happy to earn a tip bonus on top of the base 50 points. Let them stew too long and they go furious, costing you 10 points.
